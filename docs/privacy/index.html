Operand Shopify Intelligence Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 10, 2024

1. Introduction

Operand ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify Intelligence application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

We process only the minimum personal data required to provide our App's functionality. This includes:

2.1 Shopify Store Data

We access and process data from your Shopify store, including but not limited to:

  • Product information
  • Order details
  • Customer data
  • Sales and inventory data

2.2 User Inputs

We collect and process the prompts and queries you input into our application.

2.3 Generated Content

We store plans, analyses, and visualizations generated by our AI system based on your inputs and Shopify data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We limit our processing of personal data to the following stated purposes:

  • Generate plans and analyses based on your queries
  • Create visualizations of your Shopify store data
  • Improve our AI models and application performance
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Protection and Security

4.1 Data Storage

We use secure cloud storage and database systems to store your information. This includes the use of Redis for caching and temporary storage of plans and analyses.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Encryption of data backups
  • Separation of test and production data
  • Implementation of a data loss prevention strategy
  • Limited staff access to protected customer data
  • Strong password requirements for staff accounts
  • Maintenance of access logs for protected customer data
  • A security incident response policy

4.3 Data Protection Agreements

We enter into data protection agreements with merchants to formalize our commitment to data privacy and security.

5. Data Retention

We apply specific retention periods to ensure that personal data isn't kept longer than needed for the stated processing purposes.

6. Customer Consent and Opt-Out

6.1 Respecting Customer Consent

We respect and apply customer consent decisions regarding the processing of their personal data.

6.2 Opt-Out of Data Sharing

We respect and apply customer decisions to opt out of any data sharing, such as a 'data sale' or similar concept under applicable laws or regulations.

6.3 Automated Decision-Making

If we use personal data for automated decision-making that might have legal or significant effects, we allow customers to opt out of such processing.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations, subject to strict data protection agreements
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With your explicit consent

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal obligations and retention policies
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities, including automated decision-making if applicable

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
